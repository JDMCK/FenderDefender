Welcome to Fender Defender!

Our goal is to help B.C. drivers to decide whether or not the drive they are planning will be safe and to ease the stress of having to make the decision.

Who we are!

Fender Defender was created by a team of first-year Computer Systems Technology students at the British Columbia Institute of Technology in Burnaby, B.C. As a part of our journey towards learning good software principals, we developed Fender Defender as a tool to cut down on the effect weather has on your road safety. Our goal is to provide a system to ensure you can feel as comfortable as possible about the safety of your upcoming drive!
